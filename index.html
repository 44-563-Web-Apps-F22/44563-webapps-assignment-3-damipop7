My favorite Museum to visit is The Louvre

The Potrait of lisa Ghererdini, Wife of Francesco del Giocondo, known as the Mona Lisa by Leonardo da Vinci is perphaps the must see exhibit in the Louvre. Other must see exhibits in the Louvre are The Wedding Feast at Cana by Paolo Caliari, know as Veronese and the Raft of the Medusa by Theodore Gericault.
